GeoMx® DSP stands at the powerful intersection of tissue imaging technology and genomic sciences. It enables investigators to finely dissect the fundamental features of any tissue and ask critical questions concerning tissue organization, cellular interactions, and disease pathology.

In the Spatial Omics Hackathon, data enthusiasts will use data from the GeoMx platform with choice favorite dev tools to build smarter solutions to develop insights into kidney disease. You might create a graphical representation of data through visual components such as maps, graphs, or other visual formats (e.g. tools that display trends or patterns). You could create data analysis solutions that collect, interpret, and present data (e.g. tools that identify relationships or detect anomalies).
